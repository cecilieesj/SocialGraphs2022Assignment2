[[City]]\n}}\n\'\'\'Thane\'\'\' ({{IPA-mr|ˈʈʰaːɳeː|lang}}; also known as \'\'\'Thana\'\'\', [[List of renamed Indian cities and states#Maharashtra|the official name until 1996]]) is a metropolitan city in [[Maharashtra]], [[India]]. It is situated in the north-eastern portion of the [[Salsette Island]]. Thane city is entirely within [[Thane taluka]], one of the seven talukas of [[Thane district]]; also, it is the headquarters of the namesake district. With a population of 1,841,488 distributed over a land area of about {{convert|147|km2|sqmi}}, Thane city is the 15th most populated city in [[India]] with a population of 1,890,000 according to the 2011 census.<ref>{{Cite web|title=District Profile {{!}} Thane District, Govt. of Maharashtra {{!}} India|url=https://thane.nic.in/district-profile-new/|access-date=2021-01-22|language=en-US}}</ref>\n\nLocated on the northwestern side of the [[list of Indian states|state]] of [[Maharashtra]], the city is an immediate neighbour of [[Mumbai]] city and a part of the [[Mumbai Metropolitan Region]].\n\n== Etymology and other names ==\nThe word \'Thane\' is derived from local Marathi word "ठाणे" meaning "Police Check Post". Thane was the next Major British Police Check Post after Mumbai (formerly Bombay).\n\nThe name Thane has been variously Romanised as \'\'\'Tana\'\'\',<ref>{{citation |contribution-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OaqWAEry-wkC&pg=PA68 |contribution=Bd Thomas of Tolentino |page=[https://books.google.com/books?id=OaqWAEry-wkC&pg=PA68 68] |title=Lives of the Fathers, Martyrs, and Other Principal Saints |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=OaqWAEry-wkC |editor2=Peter Doyle |editor-last=Butler |editor-first=Alban |date=c. 1756|isbn=9780860122531 }}.</ref> \'\'\'Thana\'\'\',<ref>{{citation |last=Habig |first=Marion Alphonse |title=The Franciscan Book of Saints |date=1979 |publisher=Franciscan Herald Press}}, cited in {{citation |contribution-url=http://www.roman-catholic-saints.com/thomas-of-tolentino-and-companions.html |contribution=Thomas of Tolentino and Companions |title=Roman Catholic Saints |access-date=9 November 2016}}.</ref> \'\'\'Thâṇâ\'\'\',<ref>{{citation |last=Cunha |first=J. The overall climate is equable with high rainfall days and very few days of extreme temperatures.\n\nIn Thane, temperature varies from 22&nbsp;°C to 36&nbsp;°C. Winter temperatures can fall to 12&nbsp;°C at night while summer temperatures can rise to over 40&nbsp;°C at noon. The lowest daytime temperatures are witnessed during the peak of the summer monsoon in July and August when temperatures can drop to about 25&nbsp;°C. Out of total rainfall, 80% rainfall is experienced during June to October.
